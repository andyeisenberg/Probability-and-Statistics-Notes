Example 3.1.7.

Let \(X\) be uniform on the interval \([a, b]\text{.}\) So \(X\) has the pdf \(f(x) = \frac{1}{b - a}\text{.}\) We might reasonably expect that the average value of \(X\) would be the midpoint of the interval. Letâ€™s check that:
